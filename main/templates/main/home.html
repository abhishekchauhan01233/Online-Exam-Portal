Ganga Online Scholarship Test

GANGA SCHOLARSHIP TEST (GST-2020) s a online - Admission cum Scholarship test for admission in B.Tech, BBA, MBA, BCA, B.Voc. and Diploma courses. GST-2020 is a National Level test will be organized by GANGA TECHNICAL CAMPUS (School of Engineering & Technology -School of Diploma Engineering) a unit of GANGA GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS, DELHI-NCR. The test will be of Multiple Choice Questions based with no negative marking.
